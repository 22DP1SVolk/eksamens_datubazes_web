About Studio Ghibli

Studio Ghibli is a Japanese animation studio founded by Hayao Miyazaki and Isao Takahata. Known for its breathtaking artistry and heartfelt storytelling, it has produced award-winning films like *Spirited Away* and *Princess Mononoke*.
